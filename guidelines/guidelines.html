sprd : predicate in a small clause

A predicate in a small clause (sprd) is the non-verbial predicate in a small clause that completes the meaning of the theme. We follow the flat analysis for small clauses such that the sprd relation stays the same for both active and passive constructions. In the following examples, "the genius" is labeled as sprd for both the active and passive constructions.
