csubj : clausal subject

A clausal subject (csubj) modifies the verbal predicate. For an active construction, it is in the subject position.

A clausal complement associated with the expletive (expl) can also be a clausal subject.


The extrapositional "it" refers to the clausal subject, "that John was here".
