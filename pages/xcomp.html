xcomp : open clausal complement

An open clausal complement (xcomp) is the clause not containing the surface subject that modifies the predicate. For an active construction, it is in the object position.

An open clausal complement modifying the adverbial predicate "soon".

When the subject of the open clausal complement is known, it is represented by the secondary dependency indicating a subject relation such as the nominal subject (e.g., nsubj).

For a passive construction, the open clausal complement is in the subject position.
