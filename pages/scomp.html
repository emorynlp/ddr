scomp : small clausal complement

A small clausal complement (scomp) contains no verbial predicate. We follow the flat analysis for small clauses such that the scomp relation stays the same for both active and passive constructions. The relationship between the subject and the predicate in a small clause is preserved by the secondary dependency, nominal subject (nsubj).
