xsubj : open clausal subject

An open clausal subject (xsubj) is the subject in an open clausal complement that is governed externally. In the following example, "John" is the surface subject of "had" as well as the subject of "buy" and "teach" in the infinitive forms.


An open clausal subject.

The xsubj relation is also used to indicate the subject of the predicate in a small clause (sprd).
