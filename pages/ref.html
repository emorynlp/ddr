ref : referential *

A referent (relv) is the relativizer referring to the head noun in a relative clause (relcl). It is represented by the secondary dependency.


The relativizer "who" as the nominal subject (nsubj) of "smart".

The relativizer "who" as the direct object (dobj) of "meet".

The relativizer "whom" as the dative (dative) of "bought".

The relativizer "where" as the adverbial modifier (advmod) of "met".
